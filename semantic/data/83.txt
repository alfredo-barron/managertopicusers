New host records for parasitic mites of the family Syringophilidae fromaccipitriform birds (Aves: Accipitriformes).
Four accipitriform bird species of the family Accipitridae are reported as newhosts for quill mites (Acari: Cheyletoidea: Syringophilidae): Megasyringophilusaquilus Skoracki, Lontkowski and Stawarczyk, 2010 was collected from Hieraaetuspennatus Gmelin, 1788 in France and Spain, and Buteo jamaicensis Gmelin, 1788 in Canada; Peristerophila accipitridicus Skoracki, Lontkowski and Stawarczyk, 2010was collected from Circaetus gallicus Gmelin, 1788 in France, and Buteo lagopusPontoppidan, 1763 in Germany.
